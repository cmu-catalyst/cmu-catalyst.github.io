Saml is an inter-disciplinary machine learning research group exploring questions which cross multiple layers of the system stack from frameworks, specialized hardware, programming languages and applications. We are part of the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington. Our group is a collboration between researchers from Sampa, Syslab, MODE, and PLSE.
